# Glo — PCOS/PMOS Wellness App

A GenZ-friendly, minimalist women's health app prototype focused on PCOS/PMOS 
(Polycystic Metabolic Ovarian Syndrome) awareness and care. Glo combines cycle 
and metabolic tracking with an empathetic AI wellness companion to help users 
understand their bodies, get personalized lifestyle guidance, and stay 
prepared for doctor visits.

## ✨ Features

- **Personalized Onboarding** — Users are asked during sign-up whether they 
  have PCOS/PMOS, and the entire experience adapts accordingly: a focused 
  PCOS/PMOS dashboard for those who do, and a simpler general wellness 
  dashboard for those who don't. This preference can be updated anytime in 
  settings.
- **PCOS/PMOS Hub** — A dedicated section for metabolic and hormonal health, 
  education, and tracking
- **Adaptive Symptom Questionnaire** — A non-clinical, dynamically adapting 
  questionnaire that changes follow-up questions based on previous answers, 
  generating a clear, printable personal symptom pattern summary
- **Cycle, Sleep & Weight Tracking (Rhythm Map)** — Visual, calendar-based 
  tracking of cycle phases, symptom severity, sleep, and weight
- **Health Pattern Correlations** — Surfaces simple, rule-based correlations 
  between logged data points (e.g., sleep and fatigue, cycle phase and mood) 
  to help users understand their own patterns
- **Lumi — AI Wellness Companion** — An empathetic in-app assistant that asks 
  adaptive, personalized questions and offers real-life suggestions; supports 
  multiple languages
- **Lifestyle & Fitness Coaching** — Cycle-phase-based workout suggestions, 
  meal ideas based on available ingredients, and evidence-based weight 
  management guidance
- **Water Intake & Sleep Tracking**
- **Mood-Based Activity Suggestions** — Ideas for what to do when motivation 
  is low
- **Day Planner** — Plan and track daily routines
- **Glo Board** — A Pinterest-style space for health and wellness inspiration
- **Care Passport** — Printable summary of past health reports and cycle 
  history, ready to share with a doctor
- **Nearby Clinic Discovery** — Location-aware specialist and clinic 
  discovery based on the user's city/area

## 🎨 Design

A soft, minimal color palette built around:
- Deep Mauve `#674D66`
- Soft Pink Blush `#EBD6DC`

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

Built using Python and modern JavaScript/TypeScript web technologies.

*Note: This is a working prototype — some features may be partially implemented 
or use mock/sample data.*

## 🤖 AI-Assisted Development

This project was built using AI tools — Claude, Codex, Gemini, replit and Perplexity — 
to accelerate prototyping and development.

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

This is a hackathon prototype built for educational and demonstration purposes. 
It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
